Common Questions

GOT QUESTIONS?

How much does a color change wrap cost?+
Cost depends on your vehicle's size, the finish you pick, and whether it's a full or partial wrap. Most full color changes run $2,500–$5,000. Send us a few photos and we'll get you a real number, not a vague estimate.
How long does a color change wrap take?+
Most full-vehicle color changes are done in 3 to 5 days depending on size and complexity. We'll give you a specific timeline after seeing your vehicle.
How long will my wrap last?+
With proper care, a professionally installed wrap holds up 5 to 7 years. How you maintain and park it plays a role in where you land in that range.
Will it damage my factory paint?+
No. Our wraps come off clean without damaging your factory paint, as long as the paint's in decent shape going in. While it's on, the wrap actually protects what's underneath.
What's the difference between matte, gloss, satin, and color flip?+
Gloss is a high-shine finish close to fresh factory paint. Satin sits in between — soft sheen, not flat or shiny. Matte is non-reflective for a more aggressive look. Color flip shifts shade depending on the light and angle. We'll walk you through samples of all four.
